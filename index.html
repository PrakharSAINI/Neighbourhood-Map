Best restaurants along the Gedi Route

It may have derived its name from the globally known Silk Route but Chandigarh's own version - the 'Geri Route' - has established itself across generations in the past nearly four decades.The Geri route is a set of streets in northern Chandigarh where youth, mostly students, regularly drive cars and motorbikes. For the younger lot, the Geri Route is almost like a pilgrimage centre which they must visit .
